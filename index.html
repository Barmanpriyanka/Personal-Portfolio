Tic Tac Toe Game

Tic-tac-toe is a simple, quick game for two players, played on a 3x3 grid. One player marks cells with "X" and the other with "O". The objective is to be the first to form a line of three marks horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Players take turns placing their marks in empty cells. The game concludes when one player achieves the objective or all cells are filled, resulting in a draw if no player has aligned three marks.
